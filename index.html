"Transfer Fee Amort" is the Transfer Fee spread over a five year period (20% per year). William Saliba has no amortized transfer fee because he's been with the club over 5 years. I am aware that the actual accounting is much more complicated, this is a rough measure.

Inspiration

This site is inspired by the great Arsenal Report website that stopped updating around 2019. I used to check it regularly during the season and decided to see what I could recreate using Claude Code.

I don't know who made that site, but if anyone ever sees this and has the ability to contact Arsenal Report I'd be happy to remove anything they would prefer I hadn't copied.

Sources

All salary data has been scraped from Capology and transfer dates and amounts from Transfermarkt. If there's more accurate data out there please point it in my direction.
